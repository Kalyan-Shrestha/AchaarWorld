About

This is where it all started!

Our lovely grandmother passed on her delicious pickle recipes and now we, the Shrestha Sisters present you the same, with more health benefits and no compromises in the taste at all. Achaar World is owned and operated by Suwa Shrestha and Lasata Shrestha.

It’s more of a tradition, a part of life, than business for us. The vibrant rustic hues, rich aromas and the zesty flavors of authentic Nepalese food that cause them to fall in love with this spice-laced cuisine over and over again.

We, at Achaar World exude the love for authentic Nepalese food through our rich diverse culinary delicacies. Our pickles perfectly suit today’s contemporary lifestyle, which seldom gives time to relish a good meal.
